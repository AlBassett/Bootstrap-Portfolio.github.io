About me

My name is Al Bassett. I'm a fledgling programmer and a barista. I've walked many miles as a postal carrier, run several being chased by dogs (not a joke).

I'm happily married to my wife, Kate. We have two cats, Noodle and Walker, and a beautiful australian shepard, Shea. We live in Cannon Falls, MN.

For fun I run 5ks (mostly for the sweet t-shirts and medals), play video games, and have recently started a small herb garden. One of my goals is to visit all of the National parks and as many state parks as I can.
